About Me

My journey to developer starts with my time in college studying Game Design. That led me to becoming a founding member of an indie game studio. Wanting to help the other team members in a greater way, I began tinkering with C# in Unity. That's when I realized I had found something I'd been missing: a perpetual puzzle that always needs solving.


I decided to throw myself into studying web development full-time. My path continues to lead me through HTML, CSS, JavaScript, C#, and beyond. This is what I want to do now. I'm eager and motivated to solve problems with code and learn from those who came before me.
